Our Organisation

Larsatt Resources was established in 2008 and is a registered company with the Corporate
Affairs Commission (C.A.C) with RC number 819113.

The company commenced operations in 2009 as a consultancy and travel agency, rendering professional services that include Travel and Tours, Brand Consultancy and has since expanded its service horizon to include Corporate Communications, Accounting and Auditing, and Environmental Management and Sustainable Development Services.

Our traditional business model is to:

  • Help our clients in their business growth process and sustainability
  • Help businesses enjoy wider exposure to encourage more profitability by providing business solutions in aspects where we are specialists

Our reputation has been reliable, unbiased and straightforward advice, consulting and implementation in areas of strategy, management, assessment, training and support in the thematic areas.

With a deep resource pool of professionals, we combine technical excellence with an indepth knowledge of our areas of activities. Our advice Is always practical, and tailored to each project need.

By sharing responsibility and working as part of your team, we help you realise your mission.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing a ‘one-stop-shop’ solution for environmental, integrated waste and climate change management, brand management & corporate communications, and, accounting & auditing services to the Government and sector players; to assist them in meeting the strict demands of the local/ international regulatory bodies, as well as complying with the internal values and operating standards of Nigeria and best practice nations.

Our Specific Objectives

To assist public, multi-lateral and private sector agencies in the conceptualization, design and development of sound, safe and sustainable environmental engineering, environmental management & environmental health models

To assist governments and private agencies to formulate and develop capacities that will assist in the participation of the local economy in global environmental resource developments and markets.

To assist public and private agencies develop and implement sound public health and safety policies

To stand out as a first-class provider of exceptional professional consultancy services for businesses at all levels in Nigeria.

To render business solutions by assisting our clientele through professional consultancy in Brand Management and Corporate Communications, Accounting and Environmental Consultancy through innovation, advanced technology and efficient manpower.

ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT AND CLIMATE CHANGE

Larsatt Resources is management consultancy active in the fields of Climate change, Environmental Engineering, Environmental Management, and Public/Environmental Health.

The firm visualised the growing need for African firms to fully participate and maximize their capabilities in the growing fields of environmental management and climate change. The firm is built on a foundation of absolute understanding of the local problems in Africa (Nigeria) but with a deep resource base of internationally trained and experienced personnel.

Our Scope of Environmental Services

Environmental Management Policies, Plans: Environmental and Social Impact Assessments (ESIAS), Environmental and Social Management Plans (ESMPs), Environmental Conservation Policies, Waste Management Plans (WMP), Health Care Policies, site assessments and remediation, Environmental Audits etc.

Environmental Health: Risk and Impact assessments, Epidemiology, Biostatistics, Population Toxicological Studies, Trainings, Implementation and management, Remediation, HSE audits and trainings, Baseline data collection/collation and analysis

Environmental Engineering: Pre-feasibility and Feasibility studies for environmental impact of construction works. Review of engineering designs for compliance with environmental regulations.

Climate Change: Climate change research, adaptation mechanisms, climate change mitigation plans and resilience. Climate change awareness and sectoral development.

Environmental Management: Stockholm convention, Basel convention, Rotterdam convention etc., Environmental restoration, waste management, processes, decontamination and recovery, regulatory compliance audit, community relation/public outreach.

Clean Development Mechanisms (CDM): International Legal Instruments- Kyoto protocol, Montreal protocol, Marrakech Accord, Market based Instruments- emissions, trading, emissions reduction from joint implementation, emissions reduction via CDM, sustainable funding for CDM
